The Atelier

Four centuries of craft in every garment

Our studio in Mumbai brings together master craftspeople from across India. Each piece is constructed by hand, through processes refined over generations.

01

Hand Embroidery

Each piece is embroidered by hand — zari, zardozi, and thread-work applied stitch by stitch under natural light.

02

Fabric Selection

We source silks, organzas, and velvets from Banaras, Kanchipuram and Surat — selecting only what passes the hand-feel test.

03

Draping

Every silhouette begins on the body, not on paper. Our drapers work directly with the cloth until the fall is exactly right.

04

Final Fitting

We insist on at least two in-person fittings. The garment is adjusted to the bride, not the other way around.
